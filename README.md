# NuzzleMate
NuzzleMate 取名自 nuzzle，意为用头或脸轻轻依偎、蹭向所爱之物。这个名字象征着宠主与宠物之间亲密、柔软、信任的陪伴关系。
本项目旨在构建一个基于大模型的智能宠物健康管理与辅助诊疗系统，支持图文语音多模态问诊、宠物健康档案、日常用药与喂食管理、异常风险识别、健康科普、线上兽医承接与线下就医建议。
NuzzleMate 不试图替代专业兽医，而是希望成为宠主身边一个可靠、温柔、可信赖的 AI 宠物健康伙伴，在宠物出现异常时帮助用户更快判断风险、更清楚地了解下一步行动，并持续管理宠物的长期健康。

NuzzleMate is inspired by the word nuzzle, meaning to gently press or rub one’s head or face against someone beloved. The name reflects the warm, tender, and trusting bond between pets and their owners.
This project aims to build an LLM-based intelligent pet health management and assisted triage system. It supports multimodal consultation through text, images, and voice, pet health records, medication and feeding management, abnormality detection, health education, veterinarian handoff, and offline care guidance.
NuzzleMate is not designed to replace professional veterinarians. Instead, it strives to become a reliable, gentle, and trustworthy AI companion for pet owners—helping them assess risks, understand the next steps, and continuously manage their pets’ long-term health.
