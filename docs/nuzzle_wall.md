# 🐱 Nuzzle Wall | 贴贴祝福墙

Welcome to the Nuzzle Wall.

This is a small community corner for NuzzleMate.  
You can share a photo of your cat and write a blessing for this project.

欢迎来到 NuzzleMate 的贴贴祝福墙。  
你可以在这里上传自己的小猫照片，并写下一句祝福。

## How to Join | 如何参与

1. Click the button below.
2. Upload your cat photo.
3. Write a blessing.
4. Submit the issue.

点击下面的按钮，上传小猫照片，写下祝福，然后提交 Issue。

[Share Your Cat Blessing](https://github.com/1ASunny/NuzzleMate/issues/new?template=cat-blessing.yml)

## View All Blessings | 查看所有祝福

[View all cat blessings](https://github.com/1ASunny/NuzzleMate/issues?q=is%3Aissue%20label%3Anuzzle-wall)

## Privacy Notice | 隐私提示

All submitted photos and text will be publicly visible on GitHub.  
Please do not upload photos containing private information, addresses, phone numbers, or human faces if you do not want them to be public.

所有提交的照片和文字都会公开显示在 GitHub 上。  
请不要上传包含私人信息、地址、手机号，或你不希望公开的人脸照片。
